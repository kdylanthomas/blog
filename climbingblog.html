Where can I go rock climbing around here?

Sport Climbing

There are lots of places to climb sport routes in Tennessee. Popular areas include Foster Falls and the Obed Wild and Scenic River.

Other areas you can visit are:

  • Deep Creek Canyon near Chattanooga
  • King's Bluff in Clarksville, TN
  • Red River Gorge in eastern Kentucky

Trad Climbing

If you would rather place your own gear, there's plenty of traditional climbing in Tennessee. Tennessee Wall on Signal Mountain, overlooking the Tennessee River, has some of the best trad climbing in the Southeast.

Popular routes include:

  • Finger Lockin' Good - 5.10c
  • Golden Locks - 5.9
  • Mrs. Socrates - 5.12a

Bouldering

If it's too cold out for sport climbing, you can always go bouldering. Little Rock City outside Chattanooga provides world-class bouldering.

Gym Climbing

If the weather sucks, there's always Climb Nashville. There are two locations: one bouldering facility in East Nashville and a larger sport climbing facility on Charlotte Pike.
